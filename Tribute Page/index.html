Singer Dua Lipa

The prettiest singer

Here's a little time line of Dua Lipa's life:

  • 1995 - Born in the Westminster district of the City of London
  • 2013 - Signed a contract with Tap Management, managed by Ben Mawson and Ed Millett
  • 2015 - He published his next single, "Be the One", with which he rose to fame abroad, reaching the top positions of popularity in several European countries, including number 1 in Belgium, Poland and Slovakia.
  • 2016 - Collaborated with Jamaican singer Sean Paul
